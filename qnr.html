ZEN questionnaire

- How do you feel today? (on a scale of 1-10 OR 'depressed, sad, moderate, happy, exhilirated')
- How much sleep did you get? (in hours; 0 to 8+)
- How many people did you meet today? (0 to 10+)
- How much water did you drink today? (in litres; 0 to 4+)
- How much time did you spend on physical activity today? (in minutes; 0 to 60 mins+)
- Did you take enough time for yourself today? (yes or no)
- How energetic did you feel today? (on a scale of 1-10)
- How fidgety did you feel today? (on a scale of 1-10)
- How angry did you feel today? (on a scale of 1-10)
- How sad did you feel today? (on a scale of 1-10)
- How anxious did you feel today? (on a scale of 1-10)
- Did you finish all of your tasks for today? (yes, almost or no)
